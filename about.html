My Background

My passion for technology began in high school, inspired by films like The Matrix and, more personally, by my father, who was always hands-on with computers—fixing, building, and exploring how they work. That early exposure sparked a deep curiosity that has continued to shape my journey.

After matriculating, I was admitted to the University of Limpopo to study Computer Science. Although I was unable to complete the degree due to financial challenges, I remained committed to growing my skills and knowledge in the tech space.

During this period, I enrolled in the FNB App of the Year course, where I was introduced to full-stack development. This experience strengthened my interest in building applications and gave me practical, hands-on skills. I successfully completed the program and received my certificate.

To further expand my expertise, I completed a Cybersecurity course offered by Nemisa through Coursera, gaining foundational knowledge in securing systems and understanding digital threats. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computing online, continuing to develop my skills and work towards becoming a well-rounded software developer.
